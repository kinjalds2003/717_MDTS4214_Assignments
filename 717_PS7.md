9:We observe that Elastic Net with a 0.5 division rate works the best. Hitters dataset has correlated predictors (e.g., years of experience, career hits, career runs are all related). LASSO tends to arbitrarily pick one from a group of correlated predictors and zero out the rest. Elastic Net handles this better by keeping groups of correlated predictors together, which leads to better generalisation.

The prior question denotes the factors that contribute most to the overall salary decision. RMSE is ~414, that means predictions are off by about $414K on average.
